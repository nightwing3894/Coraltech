Welcome to CoralTech

Welcome to the official website of the CoralTech team! We are a team of young people passionate about robotics and engineering, ready to accept the annual challenges of the FTC (First Tech Challenge) competition. Being our first season, we want to get together to design, build and program a robot to handle a series of innovative and challenging missions. We work together, developing our skills and constantly learning new techniques and technologies.

Our objective? Let's push our limits and refine our robot to be competitive, creative and efficient. In this journey, we want to inspire other young people to join the world of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) and discover and develop their passion for robotics.

About Us

We are CoralTech, a team of young enthusiasts passionate about robotics, technology and innovation, who joined forces to participate in the First Tech Challenge competition. Together, we develop both our technical competencies and our collaborative and problem-solving skills. With each FTC season, we learn to push our limits, experiment with new ideas, and achieve our goals.

Our team is comprised of programmers, engineers, designers, and strategy members, each with a critical role in our success. Each member contributes ideas and creativity, and collaboration is the key that allows us to build a high-performing and innovative robot.
